Project 3 - MapleStory's Pianus Fight

How to play

Defeat the boss by attacking it, while dodging the boss's attacks.

Score will be decided from the amount of damage you deal to the boss, minus the damage taken from the boss.

Controls

Movement: (Keyboard Input)

  • W - Jump
  • A - Move Left
  • D - Move Right
  • H - Attack (Swipe your claws, locks you in place as the animation happens)
  • J - Howl (Used to buff the player's damage for a short period of time)
